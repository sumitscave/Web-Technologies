Aloo Paratha Recipe

If you’re a fan of bread and potatoes, you’re going to love this Punjabi Aloo Paratha or Aloo ka Paratha. Sometimes spelled Alu Paratha, this potato stuffed flatbread is a popular Indian breakfast that combines two of my favorite starches in a single delectable package. Crisp on the outside and tender on the inside, this humble meal is as comforting as a hug. What’s not to love?

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Potatoes
  • Spices
  • Ginger and green chili
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee:
  • Water

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add whole wheat flour and a pinch of salt.
  2. Gradually add water and knead into a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, combine mashed potatoes with ginger, green chili, and all the spices.
  5. Add salt as per taste and mix well.
  6. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each into a small ball.
  7. Cook the Paratha
